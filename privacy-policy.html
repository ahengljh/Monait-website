Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Commitment: Monait is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal financial data. Your information remains under your control at all times.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Monait ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is managed.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Monait is designed to respect your privacy. We do NOT:

  • Collect any personal financial data
  • Access your transaction history or account balances
  • Store your receipts, bills, or financial documents
  • Track your spending habits or financial behavior
  • Collect personal identification information
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share or sell any user data

3. How Your Data is Stored

3.1 Local Storage

All your financial data entered into Monait is stored locally on your iOS device. This includes:

  • Transaction records
  • Income and expense entries
  • Scanned receipts and documents
  • Categories and tags
  • Financial analysis results

3.2 iCloud Backup

Your data is backed up through Apple's iCloud service if you have iCloud backup enabled for the App. This backup is:

  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Accessible only with your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policies
  • Not accessible to Monait developers

4. AI and Data Processing

4.1 Apple Intelligence (On-Device)

On supported devices with Apple Intelligence:

  • All AI processing occurs locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Analysis results remain on your device

4.2 External LLM Providers

For users who choose to use external AI services:

Scenario Data Handling User Control
Pro users with custom LLM Data sent to user-specified provider Full control over provider choice
Regions without Apple Intelligence Data sent to developer-selected provider (with consent) Must explicitly consent; can opt out
User declines external processing No data sent externally AI features disabled; all other features available

Important: When using external LLM providers, only the specific data needed for the requested analysis is sent. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external provider you choose to use.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Local Encryption: Data stored on your device uses iOS security features
  • No Remote Access: We have no ability to access your device or data
  • Secure Communication: When external LLM providers are used, data is transmitted using encrypted connections
  • Minimal Data Exposure: Only necessary data is processed for requested features

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Access all your data directly within the App
  • Export your data at any time
  • Transfer data between devices using iCloud

6.2 Deletion

  • Delete individual entries within the App
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the App
  • Remove iCloud backups through your device settings

6.3 AI Features Control

  • Choose whether to use AI features
  • Select your preferred LLM provider (Pro users)
  • Opt out of external processing at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Monait is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App requires users to be of legal age to manage their own financial affairs.

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Apple Services

The App integrates with the following Apple services:

  • iCloud: For data backup (optional)
  • Apple Intelligence: For on-device AI processing (where available)

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

8.2 External LLM Providers

If you choose to use external LLM providers, please note:

  • Each provider has its own privacy policy
  • We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices
  • We recommend reviewing provider policies before use
  • You can discontinue use of external providers at any time

9. International Data Transfers

When using external LLM providers:

  • Data may be processed in different countries
  • Transfer depends on your chosen provider's location
  • You consent to such transfers when selecting a provider
  • You can avoid international transfers by using only on-device features

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications
  • Updates to the "Last Updated" date

11. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers:

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it
  • iCloud backups are retained according to Apple's policies
  • External LLM providers may have their own retention policies
  • We recommend regularly reviewing and managing your data

12. Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect personal information as defined by CCPA, these rights primarily relate to your control over data on your own device.

13. European Users

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • We comply with GDPR requirements
  • We do not collect or process personal data
  • You maintain full control over your information
  • External LLM processing requires explicit consent

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled:

Email: contact@jinheng.me

When contacting us, please do not include any personal financial information in your communications.

15. Acknowledgment

By using Monait, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms regarding the handling of your information.
